# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 28 January 2026

Orbital Drift is a premium arcade shooter. This policy explains what data is handled when you use the app.

## Data We Collect

We do **not** collect, store, or share personal data on our own servers.

## Services Used

The app can use Apple services to provide features:

- **Game Center** for leaderboards and achievements
- **StoreKit / In‑App Purchases** for optional purchases

These services are provided by Apple and may process account and usage information according to Apple’s privacy policies.

## Analytics & Tracking

We do not use third‑party analytics or advertising SDKs.

## Children’s Privacy

The app is not specifically directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy over time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

## Contact

Need help or have privacy questions? [Open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/designspin/orbital-drift/issues)

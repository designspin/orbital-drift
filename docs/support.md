# Support

## Controls

**Touch (Mobile):**
- Left side: Turn (virtual stick)
- Right side: Fire and Thrust buttons
- Swipe right: Toggle shield
- Handedness can be swapped in Settings

**Keyboard (Desktop):**
- Left/Right arrows or A/D: Turn
- Up arrow or W: Thrust
- Space or X: Fire
- Down arrow, S, or Z: Shield

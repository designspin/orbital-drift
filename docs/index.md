## Support

### Controls

**Touch (Mobile):**
- Left side of screen: Move ship
- Right side of screen: Fire
- Hold anywhere: Activate shield

**Keyboard (Desktop):**
- Arrow keys or WASD: Turn and thrust
- Space or X: Fire
- Down arrow, S, or Z: Shield
